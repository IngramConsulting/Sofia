About me

Hi! I'm Sofia Grassivaro, a professional hair and makeup artist with a passion for innovation and transformation. I graduated from Arts University Bournemouth specializing in fashion, editorial, prosthetics, and hairstyling. I take inspiration from artists and art movements, focusing on the patterns and shapes seen in the world around me to embed within my work. I love to collaborate with designers, brands and photographers to create withing the fashion and film industry.

As a make-up artist, I focus on clean, natural make-up looks necessary for skincare brands as well as crazy makeups and hair styles seen within the fashion industry. One of my main goals as a make-up artist is to incorporate more the use of prosthetics in fashion runways.
